MY BIOGRAPHY

Nurudeen Omoniyi TAJUDEEN

I was born on September 21, 1997, in Kaduna State.

My parents worked tirelessly to provide for our family, often working long hours and multiple jobs to make ends meet.

Despite the challenges, they instilled in me the importance of hard work, education, and perseverance.

Growing up, I was the second born of three siblings, and our family was close-knit.

After completing my high school education, I faced a major challenge of not gaining admission into the university for five years despite having a good JAMB score. However, I never gave up and eventually gained a scholarship to study BSc. Information Technology at the International Open University.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science Education at Alhikmah University, while also working towards becoming a Software Engineer. Being the class representative for the past years has equipped me with leadership skills that I hope to continue to develop in the future.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and learning new courses. I also love giving people beneficial information, as it really makes me happy to help others.

Another significant turning point in my life was when I gained the scholarship to study at the International Open University, which opened up many opportunities for me.

As for my personal life, I am still a student focused on pursuing my education, but I hope to start my own business and give back to the community through philanthropic endeavors in the future.
